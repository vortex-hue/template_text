Recommendations on Creating a Successful Landing Page with the Elementor Pro Templates

  • Use more requests and call-to-action (CTA). This will help your visitors to understand what you need from them and stimulate their actions. Make sure that all CTA simple and identifiable. Mainly, one may find the CTA in the form of the large bright button with the messages like sign up, register, download. While creating the CTA consider the type of audience to ensure that you have chosen the right wording.
  • Do not make the landing page overwhelmed with the information, as people will not catch the main idea and the key information. Remember, brevity is the soul of wit. So, make the web page simple but informative, and you will make the required influence on the web visitors.
  • Good structure and organized design will help to present the information. Besides, the landing page anatomy should be logical to prevent any confusion.
  • The very first element you see on the landing page is above the fold. I think that there is no need to remind you that it should be especially eye-catchy and interesting. From the very first sight, people should understand what the company is offering and the contact form.
  • Please pay attention to the footer as it performs an important role in conversions. Visitors can just scroll the middle part of the page and stay some more seconds in the footer, so use such time wisely and add the contact form, social media links, forms for the newsletter sign up, etc.
  • Make the content persuasive. It would help if you convinced people that they need a particular item. Here include the product description, principal characteristics, benefits of using, and how people benefit from using such products or services. Do not neglect using visuals to support the written content.
  • Do not forget about the encouragement. In other words, give people something in return for following a previous call to action. It will help to build customer loyalty as it will lead to a higher conversion rate. The aim of the CTA and reword is to build trust between you and the clients to stimulate further conversation. In such a case it is better to provide people with discounts rather than just a gift card. A gift card is a great idea, but it will not guarantee further purchases. At the same time, the discounts help to encourage additional investments.
  • Do not forget to use social proof to build trust and reliability. It might be positive feedback or ranking from the satisfied customers, or it might be the press coverage from trusted and well-known publications.
