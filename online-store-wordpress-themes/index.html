Licenses Types

Once you've decided on a WordPress store template, note that there are different license options. Accordingly, the purchase price is also different.

There are three licenses to choose from:

  • Personal. This option of cooperation is the most affordable. This tariff is the basic level for personal use. That is, the buyer can install the layout for only one website. At the same time, he won't be able to resell the result of his work (the final finished portal).
  • Commercial. This tariff isn't much more expensive but has a significant advantage. Customers may create up to five ready-made websites using the purchased theme. Further, buyers may sell the ready-made portals. This tariff is suitable for small web studios and marketing and advertising agencies creating and promoting portals of varying complexity.
  • Business. This tariff allows you to create many finished projects (up to 50 pieces) and then resell them to your customers. The purchase price is higher than the Commercial. However, if you approach the choice deliberately, the investment will surely pay off several times. After all, some themes include many layouts for sites of different subjects at once.
