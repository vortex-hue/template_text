However, those who have no experience using design programs, such as Adobe InDesign, might have difficulties creating their CV from scratch.

Writing a resume: what to include

Your resume is one of the most important documents in job research. The primary goal of a CV is to help the hiring managers to understand who you are, what your competencies are, and how you can help them achieve their business goals.

If your resume cannot communicate this information, youll be out of the selection process almost immediately.

To help you out, below we listed some of the essential sections that shouldnt miss in your document.

Personal and contact information

This section should at least include your name, surname, email address, and phone number. In some cases, you can also include your birth date and your physical address. If you own it, you can also decide to add a link to your portfolio or professional website.

Resume summary or objective

Your resume summary or objective section should include a few sentences summarizing who you are and what you are looking for.

Summary and objective are not the same. The summary will be helpful when you have multi-year work experience that you can summarize. The objective is more helpful for new entries and those who have limited experience.

Work history and experience

The work history is one of the most important sections of your resume. This is the section the hiring managers will look at more attentively. Here, you should include all your relevant work experiences, beginning with your most recent or current job.

For each work experience, you should include the following information: job title, period, employers name, responsibilities, and accomplishments.

Educational history

The educational history section should include all the most recent and relevant education information based on your experience level.

Ensure to include at least the following information: your school name, location of your school, the field of study, your GPA, graduation year.

Skills section

The skill section should include both soft and hard skills. You can also include any certification youve got.

To be relevant and useful, the hard skills you list should be relevant for the job youre applying for.

What not to include in a resume

When writing the sections of your resume, other than thinking about what to include, you should also think about what not to include. Some information is, in fact, irrelevant and useless and can make the reviewer lose interest in you.

To make it clearer, below, we listed some actions and information to avoid.

Too much information

When writing your resume, you might be tempted to add too much information and details about yourself. You might think that adding more information will give you more chances to convince the hiring manager to invite you to an interview; however, that's not right.

Too much information will make your CV look unprofessional and messy, and this may confuse the reviewers. Thus, make sure to include only the most relevant and essential information.

Walls of text

Walls of text are not easy nor pleasing to read and view. Thus, try to avoid them by using space and bullet lists. This way, youll improve the readability and make your document look tidier.

Spelling mistakes and grammatical errors

Always remember to double-check your CV before your application. Spelling mistakes and grammatical errors can compromise your success in the hiring process. Thus, read your content attentively, and if youre not sure, tell another person to check it as well or use free software, such as Grammarly.

Unnecessary information

Dont include information that is not relevant to the job role youre applying for. Its a good idea to include your hobbies, but dont exaggerate with the details. Other than making your document overloaded with information, unnecessary information may bore the readers.

Inaccuracies about your experiences and qualifications

When writing about your experiences and qualifications, be as much accurate as you can. Check that these sections include all the most relevant information and what youve effectively done. Dont include confusing details or information that doesnt correspond to the truth.

Most popular resume builders

Resume builders are interactive online resume templates that allow you to build a structured CV and plug in the information.

Currently, there are many resume builders you can use. This explains why choosing one instead of another can be difficult. To help you out, we listed some of the most popular online tools you can use to build your CV.

Zety

Zety is one of the most popular resume builders. Its success is probably related to its ease of use. It offers a wide range of customization options like font size, colors, date formatting, and much more. It also allows you to add links to your social media profiles.

Unfortunately, Zety doesnt allow you to download your resume for free. However, you can create a link for your resume (by clicking on Online resume), open it in a browser, and then save it from there.

My Perfect Resume

My Perfect Resume distinguishes from the other resume builders because it allows you to search skills by job title and add them with just one click. Plus, it enables you to add certifications, accomplishments, and affiliations.

Whats more, this website allows you to download your resume for free in different formats, such as PDF and TXT.

CakeResume

CakeResume is a drag-and-drop resume builder. It offers a wide range of pre-made sections that you can select and drag to build your CV. This lets you imagine how easy and quick itll be to create your professional document.

The website enables you to download only a free resume. If you want to download over one, you need to pay for a plan which ranges from $4.95 to $21.95.

Resume Genius

Resume Genius is one of the simplest resume builders currently available online. It will ask you a series of multiple-choice questions about your experiences, skills, and academic background, and youll only need to fill in the blanks.

Unfortunately, this builder doesnt allow you to download your CV for free. You can either buy a 14-days trial for $1.95 or subscribe for a month.

Canvas resume builder

Canvas resume builder allows you to create a CV that stands out and with a unique design. Most of the templates are free, and you can filter them by categories, such as Graphic Design, Corporate, or Creative. You can choose one of them and edit them by adding your personal and professional information. You can decide to change colors, fonts, images, and every element of the document.

Once youve completed your resume, you can download it for free, choosing the file format you prefer or need according to the intended use.

Resume templates Q&A

What is a resume?

A resume is a professional document that provides information about your work and academic experiences, and skills.

What is a resume template?

A template provides the design and structure for a resume. Youll only need to add your personal information.

Whats the difference between chronological and functional resumes?

Chronological resumes emphasize a candidate's past experience, whereas functional resumes prioritize their future potential.

Whats the difference between a resume and a cover letter?

The main difference between a resume and a cover letter is that the resume proves your experience and your competencies, while a cover letter is an opportunity for you to explain why youre qualified for the job youre applying for and why they should choose you.

How many pages should a resume be?

Ideally, a resume should be one page, at a maximum of two.
