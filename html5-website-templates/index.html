Tips to improve website layout

Usability / Accessibility:

  • Use a logo: link to the home page. Sounds simple, but it is a great time saver for your visitors.
  • Make your links explicit: It's easy to change links and make them visible in the background of your site.
  • Make the text readable: do not bother yourself with how to cram all the text into the visible part of the page, thereby eliminating the need for visitors to use scrolling. Let the text breathe freely, increase the line spacing and add white space. And feel free to increase the font size.
  • Add the ability to contact you: Seemingly simple, but if visitors don't have a way to contact you, they probably won't be able to report problems with your site.

SEO Optimization:

  • Add title attribute: Add this attribute to your main menu links. This small step will increase the SEO appeal of the page.
  • Create a sitemap for search engines: There are many sitemap generators out there that will create one in minutes. By doing this, you can very quickly increase the visibility of your site for search engines.
  • Optimize your meta tags: check if they match the theme of your pages and fix them where necessary.
  • Link to internal pages: This is often forgotten, but it is worth linking from the main pages to the internal ones, which are located at the lower levels.

Content:

  • Rewrite: re-read the main texts of the site. If you are not interested in them, rewrite them.
  • Simplify: try not to use too many special words and expressions, replace them as much as possible with simpler and more understandable ones.
  • Don't overdo it: people won't be hanging around your site all day. Try to concisely provide your visitors with basic information. Empty chatter is not for websites.
