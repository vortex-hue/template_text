Via this data protection statement, we inform the general public of the nature, scope, and purpose of the personal data we collect, use, and process. Furthermore, this data protection declaration contains information on the website users' rights.

As the data controller and processor, TemplateMonster.com has implemented numerous technical and organizational measures to ensure your data's complete protection throughout the website templatemonster.com and all its subdomains. Since Internet-based data transmissions may have security gaps, more than absolute protection is needed. Moreover, every data subject is free to transfer personal data to us via direct and indirect means, e.g., email or phone support.

2. Information Types We Collect

We collect certain information about visitors and users of our websites. The type and scope of information differ according to the referral source and user status.

2.1. Information We Obtain from Authors

We can collect the author's name, company name, IP address, email address, phone number(s), photo ID, social media account URLs, payment details (billing information), and survey responses initiated for research purposes.

We use this information to provide authors with support or improve our services. E.g., this data is necessary to pay commissions, verify authors' identities, deliver advertising and marketing services, contact, support, and invoice them.

2.2. Information We Obtain from Buyers

We collect a buyers name, username, email address, phone number(s), physical location, and contact details. Moreover, we can collect, store, and use information about a buyers devices used to visit and interact with our websites. It includes information about the browser, referring source, and buyer's IP address. We can also have limited access to third-party billing details collected by buyer's payment systems (billing country, cardholder name, PayPal email address), transaction details, support queries, web analytics data, and data buyers provide when filling in forms on our websites.

We may use this information to respond to buyers` requests or correspondence, provide customer service, administer the website, improve products/services, and send technical notices/updates, security alerts, changes in policy notifications, and administrative messages. We can also use this data for statistical, analytical, and management purposes and prevent fraud, and breach of policies/terms, including threats and harm.

2.3. Information We Obtain from Partners

We collect partner names, company names, website URLs, phone numbers, billing addresses, business types, emails, photo IDs, and payment information. We use this information to work with partners, confirm their identity, contact, pay, support, and screen for risk, fraud, or other similar issues. Moreover, we use this data to send technical notices, updates, security alerts, administrative messages, and information regarding our policy changes.

2.4. Information We Obtain from Website Visitors

We collect the visitors` device type and browser, network connection, IP address, cookies, and other web analytics data. We may also collect personal information submitted by visitors via any forms available on our websites, i.e., chat, ticket system, subscription, and contact forms.

2.5. Information We Obtain from Users via Chat, Phone Calls, and other Indirect Means

We collect names, emails, information about the device and browser they use, chat transcript, and other personal information they provide during the chat or call. According to our Support Policy and Terms of Use, we may request additional details from the users during our conversation to verify their identity or for technical support purposes.

We handle the information we retain following this Privacy Policy.

In turn, Google uses this data to evaluate user behavior in general, compile reports for site managers, and provide data to services related to website activity and Internet usage. In no way will Google associate user IP addresses with any other data held by Google. If users do not want to provide Google with the browsing data, they can block cookies via browser settings. Users can find more information on Google opt-out, Privacy, and Google Analytics.

A cookie is a small file that is placed on users' devices when they visit any website. We use cookies to provide users with access to all website features as well as to analyze, personalize, and improve user experience.

We activate all cookies by default to ensure the proper functioning of our website, advertisement, and analytics. All users can customize their cookie preferences and opt out of the analytics and advertising cookies according to General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other Data Privacy Laws. Strictly necessary cookies are always enabled to ensure the delivery of the requested services and resources.

Detailed information on each type of cookie we use is provided in our cookies policy.
