Animate Your Product with Motion Graphics Templates

Static images have long become commonplace, so it is difficult to surprise customers with them. Animated pictures, where each element moves to the beat, cause more excitement and impress the audience. Clouds floating across the sky, a morning run, or smiling people - all allow you to create an engaging effect for interacting with clients. Want to start a professional project and make a positive impact on viewers with minimal investment? Motion graphics templates are the most cost-effective & professional solution! Learn more about animated photos from this library now and start working on your stunning production.

Briefly about Animated Graphics

Motion illustrations are animated photos and video footage that give the visual appearance of moving elements and changing pictures. They provide smooth transitions, fluid animations, vivid backgrounds, etc. Here are the main applications for these animations:

  • With graphics, tour films become more exciting and engaging. No more using the standard Windows Movie Maker themes – get fancy with advanced animated designs.
  • They assist with storytelling. Stop wasting time introducing something. Use visually appealing illustrations and showcase any object in all its glory, complementing the photo with musical accompaniment.
  • Animated images give clips an unparalleled level of polish. As cliché as it sounds, looking professional is crucial, and using good assets helps.
  • Visuals set the mood. Are you making a clip about hackers? Add some glitchy effects. Are you trying to convey the spring mood? Use some flowery transitions and enjoy the perfect and inspirational clip.

Premiere Pro Videos - Types & Characteristics

The library contains different types of clips for any of your purposes. Are you dealing with a promo clip to introduce a new product or looking for transitions for a presentation about your startup team? There is a solution to each of your problems! In a nutshell, these types of motion graphics templates are at your service:

  • Backgrounds are the plate for your gourmet material. It won’t make your information any better or worse, but the presentation allows you to present the material engagingly and interactively.
  • Transitions help you to seamlessly go from one setting to another with a smooth animation. Fade-in, fade-out, pages turning, images flying in and out, and more.
  • Overlays are something like backgrounds but on the front of the picture. Add snowflakes, falling hearts, TV static, and more.
  • Lower-thirds are details most often seen on TV news. That small line with the person’s name and occupation is what you’re thinking of. They vary in styles, fonts, and animations regarding your purpose.
  • Revealers are, as the name suggests, animations made to reveal something. Most often, they decorate the beginning of the clip. However, with your creativity and unique ideas, you incorporate them into any part of the clip.

How to Install Graphics Layouts?

After choosing the appropriate layout, only a step separates you from building your dream promo: installation. Don't worry! Here is a short step-by-step guide on how to install these items without the hassle:

  • Install the latest versions of Adobe Premiere Pro.
  • Start a project and add at least one sequence.
  • Buy and download a .mogrt layout from TemplateMonster.
  • Open the project.
  • Click the “Graphics” button at the top.
  • Go to “Window” and open the “Essential Graphics” panel.
  • Click “Install Motion Graphics Template.”
  • Browse to the location of your downloaded .mogrt video and open it.

To edit the theme, drag it into the timeline and click the “edit” tab. Change everything style-wise, including fonts, colors, and sizes, to bring your ideas to life and create a masterpiece.
