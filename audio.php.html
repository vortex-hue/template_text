How to edit ready-made audio tracks

Once you choose and download a certain audio track, you may want to edit it according to your preferences or needs. In such a case, you need to use an editing program like OBS or Audacity.

Below we listed and described some of the most common editing actions you can bring to ready-made audios and what programs are best to use.

Separate

The action of separating comprises isolating clippings for different purposes, such as creating new remixes. In other words, when separating the audio tracks, you can work with only the vocals or a specific instrument.

To do it, you can use editing programs like Audacity and Moises.

Add or combine

If you wish to add multiple audio tracks to your video or content, you can use a free app like MKVToolNix. If using this specific app, follow these steps:

  1. Launch the app;
  2. Add the video and all the audio files you want to incorporate;
  3. Once added, use the "Tracks, chapters, and tags" section to select all the audio you want to include;
  4. Click the "Start multiplexing" button.

Merge

Merging means join and combine multiple different audios into one file. Thus, from a multitude of files, you'll get only one.

To complete this operation, you can use one of the following most common programs or online tools:

  • Online Converter;
  • Audio Joiner;
  • Clideo.

How to choose the best audio track

When choosing an audio track for your content, you should consider some details to make or break your content's effectiveness.

Below we listed some of the most important.

Identify your primary purpose

What aim you'd like to reach through your content and by adding audio to it? What sensations and emotions would you like to transmit to those who listen? The answers should be precise and clear. Based on them, you'll probably need to choose a file with determined characteristics.

Make sure it matches your brand tone of voice

Are you a formal or informal business? Do you use a simple or complex language? Based on these questions and the answers you'll provide, you can find corresponding audios. For example, if your company is informal, you can choose funnier and lighter audios. Instead, for more formal settings, pick sounds that are soft and professional.

Choose the right format

Based on your needs and preferences, you must attentively choose the right format. Here are some guidelines you can use during the selection process:

  • For music production, use uncompressed audio files (e.g., WAV);
  • For casual listening, opt for compressed formats, such as MP3;
  • For critical listening, choose a lossless compressed format (e.g., FLAC).

Ensure it is topic-related

Normally, you wouldn't listen to holiday audio if it wasn't for the holidays. This is why it may sound weird to choose that type of audio for normal days. The same happens when you choose a sound that is not appropriate for the intended use. Thus, it's important to choose the right melody and ensure that it reflects your brand or personal identity.

How to add audio tracks to content and programs

You can place audio tracks almost everywhere. Most of the most popular programs allow you to add them to the projects you're working on. However, each of them requires different procedures for the addition.

So, we listed below the processes you should follow for every original program.

How to add audio to PowerPoint for all slides

Sounds can make a PowerPoint presentation more engaging and fun to watch, and the users or audience will be more receptive to interacting or paying attention to it. Thus, if you'd like to add an audio track to all your PowerPoint slides, you should follow these steps:

  • Click on the Insert tab;
  • Select Audio, and then Audio on My PC;
  • Select the audio you want to add and click Insert;
  • With the audio icon selected on the slide, on the Playback tab, select Play in Background.

How to add audio to Google Slides

As for PowerPoint, you can also add an audio track to Google Slides. To do it, you must be following these steps:

  • Open your presentation;
  • Click Insert on the menu bar;
  • Select Audio;
  • Search for the audio you want to add;
  • Embed it.

How to add audio to Google Docs or Sheets

You can also add audios to Google Docs or Sheets. To do so, follow these steps:

  • Open the file;
  • Click Insert;
  • Click Audio;
  • Select the audio file you want to add;
  • Click Select and Insert.

How to add audio to iMovie

If you want to add an audio track to an iMovie project, you need to follow these steps:

  • Open your project;
  • Tap the Add Media button;
  • Click on Audio;
  • Then tap on Soundtrack and tap the Add Audio button.

How to add audio to Adobe Animate

If you wish to add audio to your Adobe Animate project, you must do the following:

  • Click on File, then Import;
  • Tap on Import to Stage;
  • Drag and drop the audio file to the stage.

How to add an audio track to your YouTube video

There is an embedded audio track in almost every YouTube video, even if it is a speech video. This happens because sounds can make the video more pleasant to watch and hear.

If you intend to add an audio track to your YouTube video, here are the steps you should follow:

  • Access to YouTube Studio;
  • From the left-hand menu, click on Content;
  • Click on the video you wish to edit;
  • From the left menu, click on Editor;
  • In the audio row, tap on Add a track;
  • Select the audio and add it.

Audio Tracks FAQ

What is OBS?

OBS (Open Broadcaster Software) is a free program specifically designed for streaming and recording audio on Mac and Windows.

What is the difference between MP3 and WAV?

The major difference is that MP3 is compressed, while WAV files are uncompressed. Thus, MP3 is the ideal format for distribution and WAV for editing.

How to convert MP3 to WAV?

If you'd like to convert your files from MP3 to WAV, you can use one of the following online converters:

  • CloudConvert;
  • Convertio;
  • Freemake.

What is mastering in audio editing?

Mastering is the term commonly used to refer to taking an audio mix and preparing it for distribution. This process involves multiple actions: unifying the sound of a record, maintaining consistency across an album, and preparing for distribution.

What does an audio mixer do?

An audio mixer takes various audio sources through its multiple input channels, adjusts levels and other sound attributes.
