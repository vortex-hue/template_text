Liven up your online shop presented watches and accessories with Watrix theme. You will be able to create a modern, professional eCommerce website to attract visitors’ attraction in a few seconds using this theme, which has a stylish design and a convenient interface. It will be possible to display items by their categories on the website, inform about special discounts and much more. Moreover, Watrix theme includes a powerful Jet plugins set for Elementor page builder, which will assist you in customizing the website according to your needs and vision. With WooCommerce and JetWooBuilder plugins, you will present products and their taxonomies in the most impressive way. JetProductGallery plugin will be useful if you want to show products in the form of an intuitive gallery and let visitors view them from every angle. Using JetSmartFilters plugin you will be able to add varied AJAX filters to the pages with products. You will add comparison and wishlist functionality to your website with JetCompareWishlist plugin. JetPopup plugin will help you announce sales and special deals. Using JetElements plugin you will enrich the content of the pages with attractive elements and style them up as you wish. Watrix is a responsive theme, which will be a nice option for boosting your sales and client base.
