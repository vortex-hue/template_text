Top features every eCommerce website template should offer

When choosing templates for your eCommerce website, you need to make sure they present some important functionalities. The presence or not of these will determine the success or unsuccess of your business. This affirmation lets you imagine how crucial they are.

This is the reason that explains why we listed the top features your eCommerce shouldn’t miss.

Optimized for mobile

As you may already know, the number of users that access the web from their mobile devices is snowballing. The mobile responsiveness allows the users to see and interact with your content clearly and smoothly. If not so, they could get annoyed and leave it almost immediately.

This feature is also extremely important for Google, as it is one of its ranking factors. This means it prioritizes those websites that are responsive, conferring more exposure and relevance.

Easy to navigate

Regardless of the amount of content, a website should be easy to navigate. Thus, your website needs to have all the options (e.g., menu) that allow the users to explore and discover any spot. In other words, users should be able to access the content they want quickly and easily.

In-site search

One of the most important features that shouldn’t miss on an eCommerce website is the in-site search option. Users should make their product research straight to the point and get only the results they may be interested in. If not available, they would be forced to scroll through all the products before getting to what they’re looking for, which is annoying and can make them leave.

Payment gateway integrations

A good eCommerce website should offer a wide range of payment options and let you pay with your favorite one. This is a vital feature since it can ultimately decide whether or not a user proceeds with the purchase of the product. Thus, make sure to include at least the most popular payment methods.

Security

An eCommerce deals with transactions during which there is an exchange of personal and financial information. You have to invest in a secure eCommerce platform equipped with advanced and modern security features in such a case.

Benefits of using an eCommerce template

Some benefits drive people to buy eCommerce templates rather than creating the website from scratch. The benefits are related to the functionalities, economic, and appearance aspects.

Below we listed some of the most relevant advantages you’ll get from buying a template.

Launch for online shop quickly

Developing a website from scratch generally requires much more time than implementing a template. This is why, if you need to launch your online business as quickly as possible, the latter will be the best solution for you.

Money-saving

As you may already imagine, hiring a developer can be more expensive than buying and integrating a template. This is particularly advantageous for small businesses with a small and limited budget, which can’t afford such a cost. Instead, most of the eCommerce templates are available at lower than 100$.

Free support

Most of the website templates you’ll encounter across the web offer, in addition, free support. This means there is a team ready to answer and help you with any issue you may encounter during the implementation or use of their product. This way, you’ll save time that you’d otherwise spend figuring out the problem by yourself.

Mobile optimized and fully responsive

Most of the available online templates are already optimized for mobiles and are fully responsive. Thus, you won’t need to do it manually, which may require technical knowledge and experience. Having a fully responsive website will allow you to improve and offer a better user experience to your visitors, that will be more willing to spend more time interacting with your online shop. And the more time they spend on your website, the more relevance it’ll gain.

Design flexibility and customization

Ready-to-use templates enable you to get a professional design and a fully working online store with no development skills required. However, you may decide to edit some elements or details. In such a case, you can easily do it by using drag-and-drop tools, which are purposefully designed for beginners or people with no coding experience.

3rd app integrations

The majority of the ready-made templates you'll find online are accompanied by 3rd app integrations or are compatible with them. These apps will allow you to improve the functionalities and services of your business. Whether you want to track website visitors, add social media buttons, integrate an email marketing system, add contact forms, pop-ups, customer surveys, and much more.

Ecommerce website templates FAQ

Which is better: Shopify or PrestaShop?

If you’re a beginner with no technical experience at all, Shopify might be the better solution for you. Instead, if you’d like to have a platform that offers the most customization, PrestaShop will be a more suitable choice.

Is WooCommerce free?

WooCommerce itself is free. However, you may need to sustain additional costs for a theme, plugins, extensions, and developer fees.

Is WordPress good for eCommerce websites?

Despite the fact that WordPress isn’t eCommerce-focused, it features plugins that help you build and run an online shop correctly and successfully.

How to upload an external template to Shopify?

If you’d like to upload an external template to Shopify, you should follow these steps:

  • go to the Theme library section;
  • click on Upload theme;
  • select the file that you want to upload;
  • upload the file.

How do I move my WooCommerce site to Shopify?

To move your WooCommerce site to Shopify, do the following:

  • go to your Shopify admin, then click Apps, and then Store Importer;
  • on the import your data into Shopify page, choose WooCommerce;
  • click add file and select your exported files;
  • select the WooCommerce XML file;
  • click Continue import, and then Import.
