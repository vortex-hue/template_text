Product Mockups to Download - PSD, JPEG, AI, EPS formats

Product mockups are very popular and requested across the web.

You can use mockups for various purposes, whether to show, promote, or evaluate products.

Other than helping you achieve the aim, they’ll benefit you in many ways.

For instance, mockups offer a realistic graphical representation of how the product will look after production. This will enable you to make the necessary adjustments, refine them, and improve them. Moreover, you can customize any element of the template and replace it with your own.

Moreover, thanks to the high-res representation of the project, if you need to share your product project with stakeholders, they will have an easier time exploring and learning more about it.

However, creating professional mockups from scratch could be difficult for you if you don’t have any designing experience and knowledge of certain editing programs.

In such a case, buying ready-to-use mockups might be the right solution for your needs.

Product mockup templates features

Product mockup templates present many features that make people use them instead of creating their own from scratch. And they’re not only related to the design itself.

Listed below are some other considerations you need to know so you can make an informed decision.

Editable

Almost all of the mockups are editable. In other words, you can make some necessary or preferred changes before starting production. This will allow you to customize the original content and replace the elements with your own. Thus, you will be able to utilize professional work while aligning it with your preferences and needs.

Layered

When working with digital images, layers are used to separate different elements of the image. This means you can work on the details of the image and make the necessary adjustments.

As a result, you will be able to change only a few elements of the template and leave the rest unchanged.

High resolution

Product mockup templates offer a realistic representation of a product, so most of them are high resolution. Thus, you’ll get a professional and highly defined graphic.

Easy to use

All the product mockup templates are easy to use. You can either decide to use them straightforwardly or edit them using some compatible editing program. Whatever the decision, even if you don’t have any design or technical experience, you’ll be able to open and use them in the best way possible.

Benefits of a product mockup

Many reasons explain why people prefer to create and use product mockups. We have listed some of the most relevant advantages below.

Realistic perspective

A product mockup will allow you and your team to visualize the product before it’s produced. In other words, it helps you visualize how the product will look like in the end. This means you and people interested in your project will be able to visualize the project in deep detail and make the necessary adjustments before production.

Early revisions

By creating mockups of your product, you will get feedback before getting the product manufactured. To rephrase it, you’ll be able to identify and fix issues that need to be done before the production.

Mockups are convincing

When it comes to winning your stakeholders' trust and affection, showing them mockups is the way to go. As they’re realistic representations of the final product, they’ll get a clearer idea of how the product will look like and of all its characteristics.

Improve and facilitate collaboration with your development team

As a communication tool, product mockups bridge the gap between cross-functional teams and ensure seamless design handoffs.

The handoff process is a vital moment in product development. This is where you give developers the ultimate design, along with the assets and guidelines, and they'll need to turn your mockup into a functioning product.

Time and money-saving

When buying or downloading a pre-designed product mockup, you can save time and money. If you’re inexperienced with using certain programs that allow the design of these digital artworks, you might need to hire an expert, which can be quite expensive and require a relatively long time. Instead, buying ready-made mockups will allow you to use them immediately and pay a lower price.

Reusable

You can use the product mockups as many times as you want or need and for different purposes. This will save you the time and money you would spend on buying another template.

How to use a mockup template

Using and editing a mockup template is easy and requires a few steps to follow, saving you time and money.

Below, we've outlined the steps you'll need to take to understand this process.

Find and download your favorite template

Choosing the right template for your needs and satisfying your preferences is the first step. The web is full of templates in different formats and compatible with multiple programs that can be adapted to fit any content and any industry.

Once you’ve selected the mockup, you need to download it. Generally, mockups are saved as a zipped file. If you want to make changes, you need to extract it and select the editable file (e.g., PSD). Alternatively, if you want to use the template as it is, you can select the ready-to-publish file format (e.g., JPG).

Open and locate the smart object

The next step is to open the mockup in Photoshop or any other compatible program if you would like to edit it. Once you’ve opened the file, you should study its layers. This is an important step, as understanding the layers will allow you to replace the original elements with the custom ones.

Make the necessary edits

The last step involves replacing the template mockup's content with your own. You can edit colors, add or remove elements, add special effects, and much more.

After you have finished all the editing, save your file in a more appropriate format for the intended use.

Mockup examples

A mockup can relate to different types of products and formats. You can choose the one that best suits your necessities and preferences.

Below we listed some of the most popular mockup examples.

Mockup frame

Mockup frames are professional-looking photos of a frame in a home setting. They’re the perfect product if you sell prints, artwork, or any printable on various websites.

Mockup website

A mockup website is a simulation of how a website will look. It combines the structure and logic of a wireframe but with images, graphics, and UI elements that the final product will have.

Mockup t-shirt design

Mockup t-shirts are blank canvases on which you can design a t-shirt with your graphics, pictures, images, words, or branding. It allows potential customers to see how a design will look on a real-life t-shirt before purchasing it.

Mockup logo

You can use a logo mockup to present your logo designs more realistically and creatively. It can come in different designs and layouts, and backgrounds, such as business cards, signage, and much more.

Mockup business card

A mockup business card is a model of design that will let you understand how your business card will look like. The card consists of an original design and all the informational details typically displayed on business cards.

Mockup book

Using mockup books, authors can transform their book cover files into professionally created 3D renditions or use them in advertisements.

Mockup magazine

Mockup magazines help you showcase print assets before they go to print. They include layout, graphics, illustrations, paragraphs, and much more to help you visualize the final product in the best way possible.

Mockup billboard

The billboard mockups feature a professional and realistic design of an advertising billboard to help you refine the design of your promotional product.

Product mockups Questions and Answers

What is a product mockup?

A product mockup is a realistic digital design of an item used for product presentations and other purposes.

What is the purpose of a mockup?

During the design and manufacturing process, mockups are used to promote, evaluate, and demonstrate products.

What is the difference between mockup and template?

The difference between a mockup and a template is that a template is a pre-made design that allows users to create customized files without thinking about the layout and composition. In contrast, a mockup is ideated to showcase a product by providing a realistic visual representation.

What is the difference between mockup and wireframe?

Wireframes provide a general overview of a product, while mockups provide a visual depiction of how it will look.
