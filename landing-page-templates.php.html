Website Landing Page Templates FAQ

What is the difference between a microsite and a landing page?

A post-click landing page is a single page, and it will always be published on its branded parent website. A microsite, on the other hand, can be more than one page, published either as part of the parent site or on its own. Where their similarities exist is in their size, purpose, and life.

What should I pay attention to when creating a powerful landing page?

Follow these simple tips to create a highly converting landing page:

  • Be concise and consistent
  • Have a strong CTA
  • Provide social proof
  • Stick to the brand identity
  • Use captivating visuals
  • Ensure fast loading

What is a landing page template?

Landing page templates are ready-made landing pages that do not require you to delve into the technical part - landing page development and code writing. Designer themed landing page templates will allow you to quickly choose a bright design for any offer or promotion. If you are not a web designer, a landing page template will undoubtedly be useful for you and promotion of your business.

Where can I create a free landing page?

There are many tools that let you launch new landing pages without paying a cent. Among them are ConvertKit, Google Sites, ReadyMag, and etc. You will get a complete landing page up and running in under 15 minutes. A ready-made landing page template is another option to save your time and effort when building highly converting landing pages.
