Peculiarities of Shopify Stores

To understand the functioning and features of Shopify templates better, have a look at the characteristics of this eCommerce platform. Thus, you will know what stores this system is suitable for and how you can benefit from it.

Convenience of Use for Various Businesses

First of all, Shopify is a fast-growing eCommerce system that cares about different businesses. Thus, you can opt for this platform if you plan to run a small online store. Moreover, Shopify is appropriate for single-product stores if you are going to specialize in selling one product. For instance, you can choose from a collection of single product Shopify themes for different businesses. Similarly, Shopify is suitable for larger shops that have a great variety of products. Additionally, you can sell both physical and digital goods or services with Shopify.

Besides fitting stores of different sizes and being suitable for various products, Shopify doesnt require many skills in managing an online shop. Consequently, it is suitable both for newbies and experienced store owners.

State-of-the-Art Shopping Cart

A shopping cart is an essential stage in selling and buying products. With Shopify shopping cart, your store visitors will feel comfortable making their purchases. Whats more important, the platform has included an incredible feature for shop owners. This peculiarity is the abandoned cart recovery. It will help you to encourage shop visitors to buy goods at your store. Mainly, if customers leave the page without purchasing chosen items, Shopify will automatically detect such buyers and send them an email notification. When people receive a polite reminder, they are more likely to come back to the store and finish the process of buying. As a result, this will bring you a more substantial profit.

How to Launch Your Online Store with Shopify Themes

We have taken care of numerous details to make the process of launching your store as smooth as possible. Take advantage of our well-planned templates, choose the most appropriate design, and follow the tips below to create your perfect store.

Use Comprehensive Inventories for Managing Products

Shopify website templates contain a multitude of tools to make the shopping experience pleasant. Indeed, the most important part of an online shop is the products presented in it. Using our themes, youll be able to work with your goods effectively.

To be precise, you can arrange your products the way you wish and build mega menus. It is possible to organize items into categories and subcategories, saving the space. Additionally, you can create product catalogs and collections of products. Knowing the peculiarities of your store, you will be able to manage and combine goods in the most suitable categories.

Customize Product Pages with Shopify Templates

While launching your shop, you have to choose the way to display goods on the general product page. You can opt for a traditional grid layout, or arrange products differently. After that, it is necessary to create pages for every item. Although this process can be time-consuming, it is required for attracting visitors. A single-product page aims to provide customers with a detailed description of each product. Moreover, shoppers will be able to see all product variations, such as sizes, materials, colors, etc. Shopify store templates let you use CSV files to make the process of product import/export faster.

Upload Multiple Products Images

One of the most convincing ways of selling goods is to display various images of products. Thus, buyers get the possibility to see all the peculiarities of an item. During the Shopify theme store creation process, you will be able to add as many product images as you wish. As people dont have a chance to see a product in real life before purchasing it, images are vital for making a decision to buy. If you want to add high-resolution photos of products to your store, you can choose one of Retina-ready Shopify themes .

Adjust Product Quick View with Shopify Templates

Shopify themes by TemplateMonster include an incredible feature that will make your store more successful. Using our themes, you can add a quick view of each product. In this way, people will see the basic information about the product without having to open the product page. Therefore, shoppers will save time if the product is not to their liking. They will open information about goods when they are interested in more details.

Add Search Form and Sorting Options

The purpose of visiting an online store is to find and buy an appropriate item. If shoppers can easily find products, they feel satisfied and come back to your store. So, your aim while launching a shop should be to guarantee the most efficient and understandable search.

With a Shopify template, you can benefit a lot from the search form. With the Ajax functionality, store visitors will see the prompted results while they are typing in their query. Additionally, you can facilitate the search by adding multi-tag filtering. Efficient sorting options are sure to bring a pleasant shopping experience to customers.
