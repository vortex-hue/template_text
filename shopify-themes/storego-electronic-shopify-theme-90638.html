StoreGo - Multipurpose Premium Electronic Theme Overview

Shopify is one of the largest eCommerce platforms which is getting more popular nowadays. If you are a newbie and willing to create an online store for your business you can start creating it by using Shopify. With Shopify, you can easily build your online store in less time and make it live, and start selling your products.

Shopify themes include HTML5 and CSS3 files, PSD files, documentation, and screenshots.

You can create highly looking professional stores easily just by using a drag and drop builder, so you can build your store as you want how it should look.

And if you are starting a dropshipping business then this platform is the best option for you because it provides a 256bit high-security SSL certificate so all pages, contents, transactions,s and credit card information will be protected with the same level of security.

You can create an online store for any type of business such as a fashion store, mobile store, gadget store, jewellery store, medical store, and much more using Shopify.

Talking about features

Responsive design - Shopify themes are designed fully responsive so it looks perfect when opened from any devices such as mobile phones, tablets, and computers.

SEO friendly - Shopify themes are designed SEO friendly which helps you to rank in search engines and can help you drive more traffic to your store. You can customize H1 titles and meta tags.

Advance refine search - this feature allows visitors to sort products easily by filtering product options, vendor, collection, product type, color, sizes, prices, tags, and much more.

Blog module - this module allows you to write blogs about benefits of using your products or services, or on any topic related to your business, this can help your visitors to understand about services or products you offer, it also helps you rank in search engines and get you more traffic.

Custom slideshow - this feature allows you to create custom slider images to highlight your top-selling products, new arrival products, discount offer with call-to-action button to encourage visitors to purchase your products.

List/Grid view - this feature allows you to display your products as you want. It should look like a list view or grid view you can choose according to your website theme.

Multi-language supported - Shopify theme supports multiple languages so you can create your store as per your language preference and you can also sell your product or services worldwide.

Ajax add to cart - this feature allows visitors to add their favorite products to the cart easily without entering the product page which gives visitors a great shopping experience.

Product Slider - this feature allows you to add carousel product images to highlight specific product categories which attract visitors to purchase your products.

Multi-currency supports - Shopify theme supports multiple currencies which can help you sell your products or services worldwide.

Dynamic layout - this feature allows you to add DIV-based layout which allows a highly flexible display of content. It separates presentation and content.

Testimonial - this feature allows you to add video testimonials of your customers who are already using your products or services to inspire your new visitors to purchase your products or services.

Find products from any category - this feature allows visitors to find their favourite products easily from a search bar from any category in your store,this helps them find products easily in less time.

Social share icon - this feature allows visitors to share their favorite products to their families or friends easily on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, Etc.

Scroll to top - this feature allows visitors to navigate straight to top of the page when customers reach bottom of the page scrolling for products.

Customer Reviews Options - this feature allows you to showcase the customer reviews you got all over the internet to impress your new visitors which can help you grow your sales and scale up your conversions.

Unlimited sliders + Banners - you can add unlimited amounts of sliders or banner images to your store with the product URL in it.
