Furniture Shopify Themes FAQ

What is the difference between free and premium Shopify furniture themes?

The main difference is that premium products usually contain more features, especially visual content when only some freebies provide you with all the essential components. Plus, premium themes let you get unique designs when free templates come with similar solutions. However, if you don't mind classic designs, free products can also be helpful.

Do I need my Shopify furniture website to be translation-ready?

The feature is a must in case you want to enter the international market and attract foreign customers. We recommend you to make the shop multilingual, but making its content translation-ready would also be useful. It would also better your SEO results, as not all modern search engines work with foreign content.

What is the best Shopify theme for furniture?

There's no one and only product we would recommend you to buy, but there are specific criteria a theme should follow to provide you with an up-to-date site. It should have a clean, SEO-friendly design with comfortable navigation, content hierarchy, and all essential pages easy to find. Besides, your theme should be easy to customize and contain a built-in visual builder with different versatile content modules.

How do I customize my Shopify furniture store theme?

Today's products promise easy customization so that you won't need any previous web design experience. You may change a website's color palette in a single click with Color Theme Switcher or change among several pre-designed demos if you are looking for minor tweaks. To completely change the theme's look, you will need to navigate a pre-built Shopify Visual Page Builder. There will be many pre-designed content modules, like boxes, pop-ups, tags, bars, buttons, etc.
