How to Improve the SEO Friendliness of the Content with the Law Firm WordPress Theme?

Nowadays, when the competition in any sphere is too tough, it is crucial to make the website visible for the visitors in the search results list. It is very unlikely that people will scroll more than three pages to find the perfect company. Usually, they look through just the first page. Here is the list of recommendations, which will positively influence SEO.

  • Use at least one keyword within the 65 characters of your title.
  • Give the preference to the short titles rather than the long ones. Short titles are more understandable and positively influence the acquiring of the information.
  • Try to place the key information in the first two sentences also, do not forget about keywords. In such a way, people will save their time and understand whether they need to read the whole article or just some valuable paragraphs.
  • Try to use the keywords regularly, but no more them six times and not less than 3. At the same time, please do not make the content wordy. Make it sound natural.
  • Use links to the necessary resources like legal blogs.
  • Create content that solves clients' problems or at least helps to find a solution.
  • Do not write the information precisely to suit the SearchEngine requirements. As in such a way, you may lose potential clients as the data seems artificial and useless.
