How to Use Celebration Single Page Website Templates

There is no absolutely recommended structure for creating a "right" landing page. Here you have complete control and virtually endless imagination. However, we would give you some tips that are especially useful for beginners that will help you quickly figure out what to look for in the first place:

  • Hire an SEO specialist and copywriter. These are the two most important people in the first phase of a website. Since the landing page has limited content, its low quality cannot be overlooked. In addition, optimization errors can lead to fatal consequences because people simply will not find your site due to poor ranking.
  • Forget plagiarism! This goes for every detail, including text and images. It's ok if some of the photos you take are from stock photos, but if you add pictures of goods, they must be 100% authentic.
  • Integrate social media. For many people, popular or at least frequently updated pages on various popular platforms are one of the main indicators of a company's reliability.
  • Add testimonials from previous customers. According to statistics, more than 90% of people prefer companies with verified real reviews.
