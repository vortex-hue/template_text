Business WooCommerce Themes FAQ

What is a buyout license for business WooCommerce themes?

A buyout license is used to purchase a commercial services WordPress store design for an unlimited number of projects. In addition, it is not transferable and ensures that you are the last buyer/licensee of this item.

Do I require special software to edit my business WooCommerce theme?

The main software you need is pre-installed WordPress CMS which already comes with an admin panel. This panel allows you to make any desired changes with the drag-in-drop option. Also, we recommend that you read the requirements in the right sidebar on a product page.

Can I integrate videos into business WooCommerce themes?

Usually, you can do it without any problem, though it depends on the type of WooCommerce business template you have chosen. So, we advise you to create a Support Ticket, ensuring this before you purchase.

Are WooCommerce business themes SEO-optimized?

Yes, they are. WordPress eCommerce templates for trade-related services have valid HTML and CSS, great upload speed, and responsive designs. Moreover, they are compatible with all browsers and mobile-friendly.
