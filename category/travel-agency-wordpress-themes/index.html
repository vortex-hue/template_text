Key Features to Design a User-Friendly Travel Agency Website

Booking Plugin

If you are a tour operator who sells trips online, you should get a booking plugin applied to the site. So, the clients will be able to make bookings without hanging hours on the phone with the sales department. Booking forms also allow visitors to make an appointment with you if they need a personal consultant. This plugin is good for making user experience better.

Contact Form

Almost any of WordPress travel agency themes contain a neat contact form that lets your clients get in touch with you shortly. The faster you answer, the more credible you sound for the customers. So, if you want to build trust with them, first, make sure the form is simple and comprehensive, and, second, get back to the clients without a delay.

Blog Functionality

Every day, new information pops up in the travel industry. If you want to get your site visitors updated on the latest news, choose the template with the built-in blog and accompanying elements. A website blog is another element that is proven to enhance user experience and help you build a reliable image of your brand.

Online Chat

Online chat is a perfect solution for those of your clients who have urgent questions and can’t wait until you answer them to their request left through the contact form. Some of the best travel agency WordPress themes are integrated with an online chat which allows you to provide outstanding customer care. It also lets you generate more leads and increase conversions.

Social Media Integration

Almost all WordPress themes are characterized by social media integration. Linking the site with your social media pages allows people to learn more about you and your project, as well as choosing the most convenient communication medium.

eCommerce Functionality

Again, if you sell tours online, you may desperately need to integrate an online shop right on the site. eCommerce plugin, be it WooCommerce or Ecwid, will help you do that. So, your visitors can purchase the tour independently and pay for it choosing from multiple payment methods.
