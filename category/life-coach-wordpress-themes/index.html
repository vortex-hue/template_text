Why Do I Need SEO-friendly Life Coach WordPress Themes?

We live in a world where information is created, shared, and consumed at incredible rates. It is very hard to carve your niche on the internet if you don't know what you're doing. One of the best ways to find your place on the world wide web is to optimize your website for search engines.

SEO is a practice of making your pages look attractive both to visitors and search engine bots. But why exactly do you need an SEO-friendly website?

More clicks

According to Google, most searches end within the first results page. Only in less than 10% do people ever go to the second page. More than 50% of searches end on the first three links. If you want your website to be truly successful - you need to be in these top 3 links. Otherwise, you will find yourself stuck in the never-ending flow of competing sites.

Free advertisement

While SEO done by professionals is not exactly free, it will still save you more money in the long run. If you buy ads on any search engine - you will appear higher on the list, but each click will cost you money. If you appear at the top naturally - your profit will rise exponentially without raising costs.

Good reputation

Websites appearing at the tops of SERPs usually have been online for a long time and have proven their professionalism and reliability. Users, seeing your page at the top, will instinctively trust your brand more.

Moreover, coming back to the topic of ads, people don't trust advertisements anymore. Users are more likely to click the link that appears at the top organically than on an ad. Therefore, your traffic will be organic, and users will feel more compelled to use your services or trust your advice.

Some important SEO backend forms are already filled out or prepared for you to fill out yourself. Why Do I Need Responsive Life Coach WordPress Themes?

Responsive designs automatically adjust to the screen resolutions, and operating systems users prefer. Whether you view the page on an Android phone, an iPad, or a desktop - the website will load fast and look good. So why exactly do you need to have responsive designs?

Additional traffic

More than 50% of internet traffic today comes from mobile devices. Therefore, it is imperative to adapt to these new realities if you want to stay competitive. Users will leave your website if it takes too long to load or looks bad or unintuitive. So, you want to make sure you make it as comfortable for your visitors as possible.

Helps with SEO

One of the biggest criteria for good pages for Google is responsive design. Modern search engines know about the importance of mobile traffic and will give higher spots to responsive pages. Thus, responsive websites will always rank higher than others.

Go with the times

Responsive design technology is relatively new, and many companies are still in the process of adopting it. Be ahead of the curve by having a responsive website from the start.
