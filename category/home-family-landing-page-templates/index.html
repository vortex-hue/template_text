How to Use Household Single Page Website Templates

Using a landing page may seem easy enough compared to a multi-page website because your content space is limited. This means you need less time creating engaging posts, searching for images, and thinking out a strategy. However, this is a misconception. A landing page is a specific type of web resource that requires understanding certain concepts. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of our templates:

  • Use calls to action. The most effective buttons are "download," "buy" or "subscribe," or simply built-in.
  • Add testimonials from customers. A powerful way to influence your audience is to convince them that others are satisfied with the service.
  • Counters are an effective marketing ploy. Demonstrate the amount of time remaining until the end of a sale or the number of items in stock.
  • Present your products in a non-trivial way. Use 3D models, unusual photo sessions, motion graphics, videos, collaborations with bloggers, etc. Everyone is tired of boring monochromatic photos, so this is the best way to stand out.
  • Use comparisons. Before/after photos and comparisons with products and services from your competitors is a great marketing manipulation that is guaranteed to work with most people.
