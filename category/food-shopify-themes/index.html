How to Create a Successful Online Business with Shopify Grocery Shop Themes

There are two ways of creating a store with Shopify themes: registering an account on Shopify or using a separate hosting. The second variant is more complicated and requires deeper coding knowledge. In this guide, we describe the first way.

  1. Visit Shopify.com. To start creating your account, fill in the required information and click the "create your store now" button. It would help if you had a unique shop name. If an account with the same name exists, you will be prompted to choose a different name. On the next page, you will need to fill in personal details: address, email address, contact information, etc.
  2. Install the purchased design and upload it. The store appearance will change as desired, and you can make necessary adjustments and start adding products with detailed descriptions.
  3. Add payment methods in the account settings and set up the delivery options. This stage requires careful revision; in case of violations or serious mistakes, your account can be blocked.

When you complete all three stages, your store is ready!
