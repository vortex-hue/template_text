Website Real Estate Templates: Key Features to Consider

Google Maps Integration

This is an essential feature for all real estate websites since it allows your prospect to easily find the physical location of your office or property that is currently on sale.

TM Gallery

A stunning gallery is needed to present houses, apartments, and other buildings in the best light to attract as many potential clients as possible. They will be able to consider the property from all sides and even look into the inside to decide if it suits their needs.

eCommerce Page Template

To sell property online, you need specific functionality like an eCommerce page template. By integrating it into your website, you will let your visitors make purchases and pay online which makes things much easier.

Online Chat

Sometimes, the site visitors may have questions about your buildings on sale or need help in paying online, etc. For this, the developers have added an online chat feature to many of our real estate website templates. Responding to clients’ concerns without a delay will prove your care and make you sound more credible.

Listings

Another core feature for real estate websites includes listings. They help potential customers see all your offerings on the same page and find the one that would perfectly match their requirements.

Advanced Search and Filtering Options

Although the listing is a handy and useful tool, there should also be a filtering option to help people find the property shortly. To ensure top-notch user experience, you should also apply the search bar to let the visitors find something more specific than it is offered through filter options.
