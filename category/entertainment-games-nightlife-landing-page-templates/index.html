Landing pages are a specific type of one-page site created to sell or promote a certain kind of service or product. Their main goal is to present the content to the visitor briefly and quickly, engaging him as much as possible and motivating him to perform certain actions. With lead forms, pop-ups, or buttons built into the structure of your landing page, you can effectively highlight the main things and promote your calls to action. A landing page is one of the most powerful marketing tools and is easy to learn even for those who have no website-building experience. How to Use Leisure Single Page Website Templates

You can use a landing page for various purposes, but using it effectively is another matter. To have a powerful impact on visitors, you need to follow some tips:

  • Your copy should not be too obviously promotional. Add their advantages and disadvantages to the blocks about products or services. You can also use videos or images that can say much more than trivial text.
  • Submit testimonials from customers. According to statistics, more than 90% of people prefer companies with positive reviews from satisfied customers.
  • Use comparisons. This can be a before / after photo or comparison with offers from your competitors. Show people that your offer is the best on the market based on real evidence.
  • Another good way to convince a person that your service or product is better than others is through limited offers or promotions. Give people discounts, gifts, or run a lottery. If faced with a choice of two options with the same cost, most people will choose the one that offers something additional.
