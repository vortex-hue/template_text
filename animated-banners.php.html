What to include in an animated banner

Creating an eye-catching and click-bait banner is not as easy as it seems. Some elements shouldn’t be missed, and details should be kept into consideration.

To help you create a winning animated banner, we listed below all the essential elements you should include.

A relevant image

A relevant image is one of the first elements that should be placed and included in your banner. Users are more likely to pay attention to images than words, as you most likely already know. Plus, your content will be more memorable if you include images.

A focused message

What’s the purpose of your banner ad? You should answer this question with a strong and focused message and conclude it with a call to action.

Large and easy-to-read text

The text you decide to include in your banner should be easily readable, even when presented in a small format size. Apart from the size, you should also consider the font to use.

Color psychology

When creating or editing your animated banner, pay attention to the colors you decide to use. It is recommended to use brighter colors, as they lead to more energy, and therefore action.

Well-balanced layout

Regardless of the type of banner you're creating; you should prioritize the spacing and placement of the most important elements (logo, text, and call to action).

Tips for creating and editing animated banners

Some factors can influence the success of your animated banners. They’re both related to the creation and editing process.

To help you understand what to include and how to create your banner ads correctly and effectively, we listed below some of the most relevant tips you should follow.

Choose the appropriate size

Each advertising channel and website has its own banner ad size requirements. Thus, it’s important to choose the right one.

The following are some of the most common standard banner sizes:

  • 250 x 250 and 200 x 200 px squares;
  • 468 x 60 and 728 x 90 px horizontal banners;
  • 300 x 250 and 336 x 280 px rectangles;
  • 120 x 600 and 160 x 600 px vertical skyscrapers;
  • 300 x 600 px half page banner.

Choose the right format

Animated banners are available in multiple file formats. Thus, you should make sure to choose the one that best suits your needs and preferences. Generally, it is recommended to use the HTML5 animation tools to create banners.

Add colors

Colors are extremely important when it comes to banner ads. One of the reasons is related to consistency and compatibility. By recalling your brand colors, users will be able to connect your banner to your business instantly.

On the other hand, colors can trigger emotions. If you’ve heard about color psychology, you already know how to proceed. Choose colors that inspire feelings of trustworthiness and positivity.

Choose an appropriate typography

Fonts assume an important role as they help you convey the message you want to transmit. You should consider not only the aesthetics but also the readability of the font. Plus, always think about the consistency of your brand. If possible, use the same fonts you used across your website and other channels.

Add images

Images are an essential element for any type of banner. Using them will draw users' attention to your message and make them more interested.

Consequently, use images relevant to your ad, and style them in a way that represents your brand.

Leave enough white spaces

White space on your banner ad is extremely important because it allows any element to breathe. Plus, it will improve the readability and make your ad look tidier and more professional.

Compatibility with the network

Apart from the size and format, you should also consider the length of your animated banner. You should make it as short as possible and deliver your message instantly. If you intend to show your banner on a specific platform, make sure to check their length recommendations.

Animated banners Frequently Asked Questions

What is an animated banner?

An animated banner is a sequence of visual elements composed of moving graphics and effects to create dynamic and interactive image ads. They can be created in GIF, HTML5, or MP4 formats.

How long should animated banners be?

The recommended duration of your animated banners is 15 seconds or less.

Which software is best for animations?

Visme is one of the best software for animations.

Does animation require coding?

Coding skills are not always necessary for everyone involved with an animation project.

Do animated ads work?

Through movement, animated digital media ads provide visual appeal. As the elements on these types of ads move, they can capture viewers' attention more easily.
