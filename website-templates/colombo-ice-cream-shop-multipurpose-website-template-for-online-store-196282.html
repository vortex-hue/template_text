Colombo is a simple and modern template focused on ice cream shops, but it can be adapted to any type of shop, such as garden shop, food and drink, clothing store, etc. Colombo is a good option to sell your products online.

Compatibility:

Colombo is compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Edge and Safari. It is not compatible with any version of IE, including IE 11.

This theme is 100% responsive and designed to ensure the best online shopping experience from any device.

Theme Features:

  • TailwindCSS v3.0, HTML y Javascript.
  • Fully responsive.
  • W3C valid coding.
  • Bootstrap Icons.
  • Google Font (Cairo).
  • Google Maps (Iframe).
  • Documentation included.
  • Multi Store Ready.
  • Easy to use and easy to customize.
  • Compatible with major browsers.
  • Clean code.
  • Sliders.
  • Login/Sign up Modal.
  • Notifications.
  • Loading.

and much more...
